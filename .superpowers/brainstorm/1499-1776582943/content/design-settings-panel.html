How it works

  • Gear icon sits in the todos page header (top-right, circular icon button)
  • Clicking opens a slide-in panel from the right (CSS transition, no page navigation)
  • Panel overlays the page with a subtle backdrop dim
  • Theme rows show a color swatch + name; active theme is highlighted
  • Clicking a theme: sets data-theme on <html>, saves to localStorage, panel stays open
  • Theme persists across page loads and navigation
